# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback in good faith.
- Focus discussion on the code, documentation, and project goals.
- Respect privacy; never share another person's credentials, private content, or personal information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized language or imagery, doxxing, and deliberate disruption are not tolerated.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report concerns privately to the repository maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.
